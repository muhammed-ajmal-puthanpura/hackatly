Frequently Asked Questions

How much will it cost ?

Most of our resources will be free of cost. However, the event would involve a certain amount of registration fee of 500 per head.

Do I need a team?

You can sign up as a team of 2 to 4. Members can be from the same college or from different colleges. It's up to you.

Who can attend ?

Be a beginner or a pro, anyone of the age 18 or above who would like to explore the field of coding are welcome!

How do I register ?

Click on the registration button, fill out the form(make sure that all the details are entered correctly)and we will get you ready to code!

What should I bring ?

Bring your student ID card, laptop, chargers, and all other necessary items you need to use.

When does registration close ?

Registration will be closed sharply on 29 th of April, at 3.00 PM . So hurry up and register, and be a part of this fun filled experience, with intriguing talks ,interactive sessions and interesting games around the clock. So buckle up, y all !

Do I need to know how to code?

Coding experience is not at all necessary. Non STEM majors, first time hackers and beginners codes are welcomed and encouraged to join us . We will have mentors to guide you

Where is it ?

Hackatly will be taking place at College Of Engineering Thalassery, Eranholi P.O Thalassery, 670107
